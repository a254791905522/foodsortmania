Who this applies to

This page describes how we think about privacy for Food Sort Mania when you install and play it on a compatible Apple device. It is meant for players and parents who want a clear summary alongside what Apple publishes on the App Store.

If anything here disagrees with the live App Store “App Privacy” section or a privacy URL shown on that listing, the store page and the developer-linked policy should be treated as the source of truth for the shipping app.

Personal data & collection

Apple’s App Privacy label for this app currently states that the developer does not collect data from this app in the categories Apple asks developers to disclose.

We do not operate Food Sort Mania as a tool to build personal profiles, run first-party analytics on you, or sell your information.

Account in the app This statement does not assume you must create an in-app account to play.
Gameplay data Progress and preferences typically stay on your device unless you use Apple system features (for example backups or iCloud) that copy or sync data according to your settings.
Support email If you email the developer, whatever you choose to include in that message is handled like normal correspondence — not as part of automated in-app tracking.

Developer-hosted policy referenced from the store (if you need the full legal wording): Food Sort Puzzle — privacy policy

Advertising

The App Store indicates that the app contains advertising. Ad networks and measurement tools may process data according to their own policies and to Apple’s rules. That processing is generally described by the ad partners and by Apple, not fully replaceable by this short page.

You can review privacy choices at the system level (for example Apple’s tracking and privacy settings on your device) and read Apple’s Privacy Policy for how the platform handles purchases, updates, and related activity.

Apple & your device

Downloading, updating, and using apps from the App Store involves Apple. Network, crash, or billing events may be visible to Apple as part of operating the store and iOS. We do not control Apple’s infrastructure.

  • Review Apple’s settings for Location, Analytics, Tracking, and Apple Advertising on your device if you want to limit sharing where the OS allows.
  • Parental controls and Screen Time can help manage installs and usage for younger players.

Children

Food Sort Mania is rated 4+ on the App Store. Parents and guardians decide what fits their family; this app is not directed at collecting children’s personal information beyond what Apple’s label states for the product.

When we update this page

We may edit this summary when the app, store disclosures, or applicable rules change. The “Last updated” line at the top will move forward when we make meaningful edits. Material changes to how data is handled should also be reflected on the App Store where required.

Questions

For support or privacy questions about Food Sort Mania, use the contact options on the official App Store listing or visit the developer site.
