Food Sort Mania

Food puzzles & tasty challenges

Sort it. Stack it. Savor the fun!

Welcome to Food Sort Mania — the tastiest brain teaser on iPhone. Sort, stack, and match mouth-watering treats like burgers, donuts, tacos, and ice cream inside bright test tubes. It is built to relax your mind, train your logic, and deliver that satisfying “just one more level” feeling.

Whether you love cute food art or classic sorting puzzles, every stage is a colorful bite-sized challenge. Tap to move pieces, clear the tubes, and unlock new foods as you go — no stress, just flavor and focus.

How you play

  • Tap to move delicious items between tubes until each tube holds matching stacks.
  • Plan a few moves ahead — crowded tubes are the trickiest part of the recipe.
  • Clear levels to unlock new foods and fresh layouts; difficulty grows at a friendly pace.
  • Play in short sessions anytime; the game supports offline play when you are on the go.
